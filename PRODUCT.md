# PRODUCT.md — Looking Glass

## What It Is

Looking Glass is a **personal visual memory management and vision board system** — an infinite canvas spatial organizer that thinks the way your brain thinks: visually, spatially, and associatively.

Not a collaboration tool. Not a whiteboard for teams. A **solo thinking space** — like a infinite pinboard where your references, ideas, notes, and media live in spatial relationship to each other.

## Who It's For

**Primary user:** An individual managing a large personal visual library — references, inspirations, mood boards, notes, screenshots, PDFs, video, and audio — who wants AI to help make sense of it all.

**The job:** Capture visual memory. Organize it spatially. Let AI help tag, search, summarize, and generate design documents from selections. Turn chaotic reference collections into structured mood boards and design systems.

## Product Type

**Product** (not brand). This is a tool/app users interact with daily.

## Brand Personality (3 words)

**Liquid. Award-winning. Essential.**

This should feel like a prestigious iOS app — the kind that wins Apple Design Awards. Not cheap. Not generic. Every pixel considered. Every interaction fluid.

## Visual Direction (positives)

- **Apple Design Award quality** — precision, clarity, delight
- **Liquid Glass** — real glass refraction, depth, light behavior (not cheap glassmorphism)
- **Spatial canvas** — infinite, pan-and-zoom, items exist in physical space
- **Personal gallery** — feels like walking through your own museum of ideas
- **Fast, fluid, responsive** — 60fps, instant feedback, never janky

## Anti-references (what this must NEVER look like)

- **Glassmorphism blur overlays** — no cheap translucent card stacks
- **Collaboration whiteboards** — no Miro/Figma sticky notes, avatars, cursors, real-time collaboration UI
- **Generic SaaS dashboards** — no sidebar nav + cards + search bar template
- **Spatial memory/3D room metaphors** — no VR rooms, no object-based memory palaces
- **Broken, unfinished, janky** — no half-rendered UI, missing buttons, inconsistent spacing, buttons that don't work
- **Look-alikes** — nothing that feels like a Notion or Evernote clone

## Core Features

### Canvas
- Infinite pan-and-zoom canvas with dot-grid background
- Cards: Notes, Images, PDFs, Video, Audio, Web Clips, Links
- Dark/Light themes with warm off-white paper (light) and deep black (dark)
- WebGPU glass rendering with tiered fallback (Blur → Gradient → Flat)

### AI Integration
- **Tag & Search** — AI auto-tags items, semantic search across collection
- **Summarize** — AI summarizes card content and selections
- **DESIGN.md generation** — Select any group of cards, AI writes a complete design document (colors, fonts, spacing, tokens) describing the visual language
- **Reverse image prompts** — Upload an image, AI generates prompts that could recreate/modify that aesthetic
- **Mood boards** — AI assembles curated mood boards from visual references, with compositional intent

### Organization
- **Spaces** — Multiple canvases/projects (like folders, but spatial)
- **Tags** — Multi-tag system with filtering
- **Minimap** — Bird's-eye view of entire canvas for navigation

### Import/Export
- Drag-and-drop files onto canvas
- Export canvas as JSON, markdown, or image
- Import from JSON
